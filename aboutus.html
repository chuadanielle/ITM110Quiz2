About Us

Digital Espresso coffee shop and cafe delivers way beyond
the traditional coffee shop favorites. Our cafe offers
traditional but the best quality handcrafted coffee and
espresso drinks in Manila. The Digital Espresso first
started in Ateneo when 5 students decided to put up a JSEC
stall in 2003. After that, the demand for the coffee became
consistent as students loved the taste, quality and it's affordability.
Because of this, the owners decided to start their business outside
Ateneo and they put up their first branch in Regis, Katipunan. Since
then, the business grew and more and more customers come back for
their affordable and great quality coffee drinks.
